About Charity through Art (CHAART) Foundation

CHAART Foundation, formed in 2008, is a 501c3 nonprofit organization dedicated to raising funds for helping nonprofits provide food and education to poor children around the globe through the organization of dance and music concerts.

Key Achievements:

  • Organized ten fundraising dance/music concerts
  • Raised about US$10,000 per year
  • Supported over 20 global nonprofit organizations contributing to our cause

For more details, please visit www.chaartfoundation.org.
