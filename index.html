I was born in Trichy, India. My father was an ex-serviceman (military veteran) who fought for the British during World War II in the 1940s. As a result, I joined Sainik (military) school in India – a school for training military officers – at the young age of nine wanting to be a wing commander in the Air Force. How I ended up from wanting to be an Air Force Officer in the Military to an Academic Professor in Marketing? That story is told through a 5-min video interview by some of the young high school students I mentor – Special thanks to John Lenihan for videographing.
