Teaching

Because of my training, background, and interest in engineering, management and data-oriented marketing, I have been fortunate to teach a wide range of courses – about 18 on marketing strategy and quantitative marketing at Northwestern University, University of Iowa, Southern Methodist University, Indian School of Business, Great Lakes Institute of Management and Jio institute, as well as to executives in several multinational corporations. They are:

  • Thinking with Data in Marketing
  • Advertising Strategies
  • Market Segmentation
  • Marketing Models
  • Customer Insights
  • Marketing Management
  • Introduction to Marketing
  • Database Marketing
  • Marketing Research
  • Multivariate Statistical Analysis in Marketing
  • Working with Data in Marketing
  • Brand Equity
  • B2B Marketing
  • Price and Advertising Strategies
