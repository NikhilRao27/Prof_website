Founder-President (Since 2008)

Charity through Art (CHAART) Foundation was formed in 2008
as a 501c3 U.S. based nonprofit organization.
The sole purpose of the foundation is
to organize Dance and Music concerts
to raise funds for dedicated charities
who provide food and education
to poor children around the globe.

So far (2008-2024),
we have organized/coordinated
about 70 dance/music concerts,
raised over US$100,000, and
distributed to 12 global nonprofit organizations
supporting our cause.
For more details visit www.chaartfoundation.org
